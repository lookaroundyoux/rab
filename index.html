Our goal

We wanted to create a platform that would make it easier for students to discover and share books with one another. The website we created allows users to look at the selection of books available, request one and offer another in return.

Our goal is to create a community for book lovers who can share their passion for reading and discover new authors and titles.

We believe that by making it easy for people to access new books, they can grow their love for reading. We are constantly working to improve the site and make it even more user-friendly, as well as looking for more ways to expand the book lover community.

How does Rid a Book work?

You can take a look at our available books and decide which one you would like to be your next read. You then must complete the form in our ‘Order’ section. The book you are offering in exchange must meet the criteria listed here. After your request is accepted, you will be contacted by one of our team members and decide on a time you can meet during school hours to complete the book exchange.

Criteria

Here is the crteria any books that you want to offer in exchange must respect:

The book must be written in english.

The book must not be included in the school curriculum.

The book must be suitable for students between 13 and 18 years old.

The book may have signs of usage, but it must not be damaged.

The book must have a general rating above 2.5 stars.
